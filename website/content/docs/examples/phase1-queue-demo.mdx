description: Demonstrate deduplication, forefront priority, leasing, reclaim, and concurrent consumption.
---

The example submits 60 additions whose unique keys deduplicate to 40 queue entries, then adds one forefront request that the first successful fetch receives. Eight workers consume leases, reclaim `/page/7` once to increment its retry count, mark successful leases handled, and verify all 41 unique requests finish with exactly one observed retry.
